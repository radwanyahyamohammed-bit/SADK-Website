Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Who can join Deutsch Klub?

The club is open to all students at STEM Asyut who are interested in learning German, regardless of their current level. Beginners are especially welcome!

When are the club sessions held?

Sessions are typically held weekly during the school year. Check our schedule on the website or contact us for the current timetable.

Do I need prior German knowledge?

No prior knowledge is required! We offer materials for all levels, from absolute beginners to advanced learners.

How can I access learning materials?

All learning materials are available on our website under the "Learn German" section. You'll need to be a registered member to access some resources.
